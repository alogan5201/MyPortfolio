Pomodoro App

This is an app I built that can be used to track work session following the pomodoro technique. After completing one work sessions an image of a half-filled tomato will appear.

Once a whole hour of work is completed, then the tomato will be completely filled. 1 hour = 1 whole tomato.

Each work session is logged and categorized by Month on the archive page. On the settings page you have the option to delete all records from the database.
